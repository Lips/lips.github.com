Used bulk food bins

Approximately 46 bins, with wire supports. The supports are screwed into a supporting surface through 4 holes.

This bulk dispensing system was sold by Trade Fixtures, but has been discontinued. The bins are identical to food prep containers available at numerous kitchen supply companies. They’re made from either Polypropylene or Polyethylene, we don’t recall, but are BPA-free in either case. Snap fit lids may be available.

Durable replacement lids (that won’t crack) can be made at TnT Plasticland, just south of Canal. 4th Street Food Co-op has templates available for this. The original lid hinges were made from flexible molded plastic, but they’ve all broken, and replacements are not available. As a replacement, we’ve used small binder rings (from office supply stores) for over a year. Zip-ties were also tried, but failed on a regular basis.

  • Height: 8.25”
