Initial interview

The first project should be an initial interview, to build a sketch of the organization focus, competencies, resources, schedule, etc - to inform other work.

Event documentation

  • Media: Photo, video, audio, transcription.
  • Deliverables: Raw material, sorted/edited material, or packages, delivered via variety of media.
  • Method: Documentation would be based on specific requests, & take into account the initial interview & peer recommendations.

Event production (get terminology)

NPO advises or takes responsibility for basic event production.
NPO would work from planning documents, space & equipment descriptions to ensure quality of both event and documentation, either advising on setup, or handling lightweight logistics such as delivery, setup & takedown of projector, screen, lighting, audio equipment, chairs, backgrounds & stage furniture.

Corporate photography

Portrait and location photography of staff, offices, sites & operations, for site & other annual publications.

Equipment Loans

Basic shooting equipment: Numerous point & shoots for crowdsourcing media, SD & CF cards, flashes.  Basic event equipment: See “Event production.”

Classes

Basic digital imaging: DPI/resolution, metadata, free tools, optimizing images.  Photography: Technique & styles (portraiture, strangers, events), iphone & cam photography.  Getting the most out of point & shoot cameras.  Understanding digital SLRs.  Digital Asset Management & workflow.

DAM: Work

One-off or scheduled organization of existing “shoeboxes” of media.

DAM: Consultation

Digital asset organization workflow & software consultation.

Online services

Hosting of assets, similar to vimeo/flickr/youtube, but with more control, flexible access management, analytics.
Assets could be mirrored to services to take advantage of social aspects?
(Access management, analytics, would offer more specificity than other services, but wouldn’t be integrated into a dashboard/campaign.)

Other media services

Format conversion, batch resolution processing, subtitling, either individually, or as a step in an already existing pipeline - for materials that are being self-produced within the client organization.  Production, editing, retouching.

Packaging

Producing of EPKs, various formats, disc, web, etc.

Support

Could we offer a simple support mechanism? Twitter, helpdesk, email, live chat?
